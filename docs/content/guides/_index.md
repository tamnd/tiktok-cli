Each guide is built around a job rather than a command, and together they cover
every command in the tool. They assume you have run the
[quick start](/getting-started/quick-start/).

- [A video and its author](/guides/a-video-and-its-author/) reads one video and
  pages a user's feed (`video`, `posts`).
- [Comments and replies](/guides/comments-and-replies/) reads a video's
  discussion (`comments`, `replies`).
- [Searching and profiles](/guides/searching-and-profiles/) finds videos and
  accounts and reads a profile (`search`, `users`, `user`).
- [Hashtags and sounds](/guides/hashtags-and-sounds/) reads a tag or sound and
  the videos under it (`hashtag`, `sound`).
- [The trending feed](/guides/the-trending-feed/) reads the logged-out
  recommend feed (`trending`).
- [Discovering hot nodes](/guides/discovering-hot-nodes/) walks the public graph
  from seeds (`discover`).
- [The raw page blob](/guides/the-raw-page-blob/) prints a page's whole
  universal-data JSON (`raw`).
- [Serving and agents](/guides/serving-and-agents/) runs the commands over HTTP,
  as MCP tools, and as a `tiktok://` driver (`serve`, `mcp`).
